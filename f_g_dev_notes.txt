This game uses an increasing number of custom groups, functions and APIs across its many mods.

Groups:

- For the purpose of crafting
	- cobble
	- iron: Iron Ingots and Pig Iron

- Other
	- unsilktouchable: Ignores the silktouch functionality of the Ruby Pickaxe
	- ore: Allows the fortune capabilities of the Sapphire Pickaxe to be used on the node
