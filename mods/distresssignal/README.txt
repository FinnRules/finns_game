Distress Signal, a mod that creates the distress signal, which, when used, telports you to surface level above you, from anywhere on the Y axis. Intended to eliminate the boring gameplay of climbing out of giant caves.

Written by Emetchi, LGPLv3

Very valuable assistance and advice from exe_virus, ElCeejo and wsor. Thank you.
